Account Settings

Mail

  • Identities: Set up and manage alternative email addresses that deliver email to your account. Identities also allow you to send email using addresses that are not associated with your Runbox account.


Manager

With Manager you can easily import, filter, and forward your email as it "falls" through the levels below.

  • Retrieve: Lets you import email from external accounts (for instance Gmail or Hotmail) into your Runbox account. You can import email using both POP and IMAP.

  • Filter: Configure protection against unwanted email, set up an autoreply, and sort email to folders and other email addresses.

  • Access: Shows you how to access all the email stored on the server - with a computer, tablet, cell phone, or any other device.


Hosting

  • Email Hosting: Set up your Runbox account to handle email for your domain.

  • Domain Hosting: Manage domain names that you can then set up with our Email Hosting and Web Hosting services.

  • Web Hosting: Manage your Runbox Web Hosting accounts.

Subscriptions

  • Main Account Plans: Subscribe to Runbox, or change an existing subscription plan.

  • Add-ons & Sub-Accounts: Add-ons are individual upgrades that can be added to your main account.

  • Active products & Renewals: All the active products in your account, and renew any of them at any time.

  • Transactions: List of all your payment transactions.

  • Card Details: Details of your credit/debit cards.
